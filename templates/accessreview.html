What is Access Review?

ISO 27001 Clause 5.18 requires organizations to regularly review user access rights. This review identifies inactive accounts that could be exploited by attackers, users with excessive privileges, and accounts belonging to employees who have left. Conducting this review quarterly and documenting it here provides audit evidence of your access control program.
