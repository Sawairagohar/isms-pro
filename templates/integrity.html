What is Data Integrity and why does it matter?

📊 Your compliance score is calculated from your actual data — risks, training, policies, scans. If that data is missing or incomplete, the score becomes unreliable.
🔍 ISO 27001 auditors don't just check your score — they check the evidence behind it. Missing data means missing evidence, which causes audit failures.
✅ A HIGH integrity score means your compliance percentage is trustworthy and backed by real evidence that would satisfy an auditor.
